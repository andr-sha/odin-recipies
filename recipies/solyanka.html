Solyanka

Description

Solyanka. Solyanochka. Mwah!

It is soup with differents types of meat.

    Ingridients:
  • 3 or 4 types of meat. As raw meat you can take pork or beef on bone. Be sure to take some smoked meat - can be anything including chicken. If you take sausages be sure they are made of meat and not of some unbeknownst pulp. Otherwise you can spoil the soup.
  • Onion
  • Soft tomatoes or tomato paste
  • Lemon
  • Olives
  • Seasonings
    How to cook:
  1. Boil raw meat for needed time. You can add an entire onion to water and then just throw it away.
  2. Cut one-two raw onions into little pieces and put it into a pan. Fry it a little.
  3. Put prepared meat (smoked meat, sausages) into the pan.
  4. Add tomato paste.
  5. Fry this mix for some time.
  6. You can pour a bit of broth from your boiling meat to the mix. Many people say it makes the difference.
  7. Take out meat from the broth and cut/tear it on the fibers.
  8. Put the mix to the broth.
  9. Put the cut boiled meat to the broth.
  10. You can add some chili pepper to make the whole thing little spicy. Do not overdid with that though, it is not about flamethrowing.
  11. Add one-two rings of lemon and press them out there. Throw away the rind.
  12. Add cutted into rings olives (I prefer the black ones) and send them there.
  13. Add salt somewhere at the end if it's nessesary. Some of the prepared meat can contain salt. I like this soup just a little oversalted btw.
  14. It sure must be served with half a ring of lemon in each plate. It is also better to add some shredded fresh green things like parsley.
  15. Enjoy!
